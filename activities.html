Open water Diving Session 1
Location: Breakwater Cove
Breakwater Cove is protected by both the natural harbor and the marina just south of the entrance. It can be crowded since it is popular at night. There are plenty of eye-catching corals, nudibranchs, and macro life to keep the photographers happy!
Duration : 3 hours                      Time: 11-2 pm/ 5-8 pm
Minimum age : 13 years old      Equipment : included
Price : $380

Open water Diving Session 2
Location: The Farallon Islands
The diving scene of California begins at the Farallon Islands, nearly 30 straight miles west of San Francisco’s Golden Gate Bridge. The Farallon Islands are a marine sanctuary, home to over 300,000 nesting birds and a mating site for seals and sea lions.
Duration : 2 hours                      Time: 9-11 am/ 4-5 pm
Minimum age : 12 years old      Equipment : included
Price : $250

Simple Life Holiday Dive

Just 100 metres from Sairee Beach, Koh Tao Simple Life Resort features a diving school and full spa. Free Wi-Fi is provided. Simple Life Resort is a 10-minute drive from Mae Haad Pier. This hotel is close enough to food, entertainment, bars, and the beach, but removed so that your room and setting can feel peaceful and quiet.

Bahia's Resort Holiday Dive
Hotel Name: Bahia's Resort Hotel
The Bahia is situated on a peninsula on the west shore of Mission Bay in San Diego. Less than a 15 minute drive from the San Diego airport. Mission Bay Park, the West Coast’s largest aquatic man-made park, covers over 4,000 acres and offers a wide range of recreational activities and water sports. Swimmers and sun-lovers are drawn to the calm water and warm sand that Mission Bay offers.
Duration : 5 days 4 nights                Maximum depth : 50 meters
Diving Duration: 5hrs per day         Equipment : included
Price : $800 per night
