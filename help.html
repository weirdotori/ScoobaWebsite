FAQ
Q: What is Scuba Diving?
A: Scuba diving is an underwater swimming activity involving the use of self-contained underwater breathing apparatus (SCUBA).

Q: What equipment do I need for scuba diving?
A: The basic equiment you need for scuba diving includes a mask, fins, snorkel, regulator, computer control and a weitsuit or drysuit.

Q: What are the best diving destinations?
A: The most popular diving destinations around the world are the Great Barries Reef in Australia, the Maldives, the cenotes in Mexico and other more.

Q: How can I book for a diving course?
A: You can book directly online through the form we provided for each course. You can choose the course you like to enroll with associated package. We will send you a confirm email as soon as possible for more information.

Q: Are there any age limitations to dive?
A: The short ansawer for that is YES. You need to read the requirements we provided carefully before you enroll a course. The minimum age requirement depends on the different course with associated package. The junior level courses can be enrolled starting from the age of 9 or 10.
