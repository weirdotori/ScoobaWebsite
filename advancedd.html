Advanced

In the Advanced Package, you are required to do a knowledge review for each dive, which your instructor will go over with you before getting in the water. The course can be done over 2 or 3 days, with up to 3 dives being completed in one day. You will learn the detail structures and techniques to be expert in diving and clver ways to hold your breathe. On your deep dive, most instructors will play a little game to test how nitrogen narcosis may affect you. Our instructors will provide you tips and trips to stay longer underneath the ocean as well.




Although the advanced package seems hard, there will be your own tutors to guide and help you anytime! You will get over all the tests with our hamble instructors' supervisions. The package is designed as a last step to be professional in diving. Believe in yourself and join with us!
