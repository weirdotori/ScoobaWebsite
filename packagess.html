A course for those who want to try to breathe underwater for the first time, guided by the instructor, diving to a depth of 12 meters in complete safety. There is a theoretical presentation, a session of exercises in shallow water (confined) and then an open water dive up to a depth of 12 meters.

Intermediate Packages
At this stage, you need to know how to adjust your breathing and can hover without flailing. This is the perfect dive for you if you are not either newbie or pro!

Advanced Packages
The Advanced diving package is all about advancing your skills. Once certified as an Advanced Open Water Diver, you'll be trained to dive to a maximum depth of up to 30 meters (100 feet).

Custom Packages
You can organize your packages according to your own interest and experience levels!
